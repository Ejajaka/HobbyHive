HobbyHive Community Guidelines

Building a safe and enriching environment for skill sharing and learning

Our Core Values

Mutual Respect

Treat all community members with respect, regardless of skill level or background.

Continuous Learning

Embrace a growth mindset and be open to learning from others.

Community Rules

1. Honest Representation

Be truthful about your skills and experience. Misrepresentation damages trust and learning opportunities.

2. Fair Exchange

When charging for services, maintain transparent and reasonable pricing. Remember, we're here to help each other grow.

3. Constructive Communication

Provide feedback respectfully and constructively. Focus on helping others improve.

Safety & Trust

Member Protection

  • Never share personal financial information
  • Report suspicious behavior immediately
  • Use platform messaging for communication

Dispute Resolution

  • Contact moderators for mediation
  • Document all agreements clearly
  • Follow platform refund policies

Monetization Guidelines

When charging for your services:

  • Set clear expectations about what learners will receive
  • Provide detailed pricing structures upfront
  • Maintain professional standards in service delivery
  • Honor scheduled commitments and time slots

Communication Standards

To maintain a positive learning environment:

  • Use appropriate and professional language
  • Respond to messages within 24 hours
  • Be clear and specific in your communication
  • Respect others' time and boundaries
